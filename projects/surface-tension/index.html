Surface Tension : Interactive Installation For The STEM Building

Developer • Constructor

During the summer of 2018, I was asked to help in building an interactive installation for the new STEM building that opened in September 2018. Together with the amazing team, we worked daily on building "Surface Tension" which is an interactive wall which describes the force of attraction between individual water molecules creating a bond resistant to external pressure.

The talented team was comprised of "Keshav Deeiljur, Sarah Hodgson, Lucy Oulanova, Devansh Shah, Mohamed Abdrabou, Sarmad Nomani, Nikhil Peri, Laura Weller, and Ali Sanaknaki."

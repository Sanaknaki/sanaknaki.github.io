Projects


Jobify



Jobify was a project for my Databases 1 course, where groups were supposed to build a website that allowed students to rate their co-op jobs for other students to see, we used a web scraper built in Python to find company names, job position titles and logos for each company and insert them into our database, and used Rails, PostgreSQL, and JQuery to build the functionality of the site.

Cloutstagram was built to resemble Instagram's mobile app using Swift 4 and Firebase. I took on this project in order to enhance my mobile app development skills, and to learn more about the functionalities of XCode. (Still in progress)






Cookbook



Cookbook was my Intro. to Software Engineering final project, myself and a group worked from conceptualizing to fully developing an app that aided people in finding and creating cooking recipes. I was in charge of designing and building the UX of the application, I used Sketch to create drafts of the screens, then used Android Studio to build the app screens and functionalities in Java.
